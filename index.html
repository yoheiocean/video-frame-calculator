To find the frames per second (FPS) of a video on your iPhone or Android:

1. Open the 'Photos' app on your device.
2. Select the video you're interested in.
3. Look for an option or icon that provides video information.
4. Check the details for the frames per second (FPS) information.

Please note that the exact steps may vary depending on your device and the app you're using.
